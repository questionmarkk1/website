Sweet Potato Brownie Recipe

Indulge in these fudgy, plant-based sweet potato brownies that are as nutritious as they are delicious. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth the Arielsk8ts way!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, mashed (about 1.5 cups)
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips (vegan)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed sweet potatoes, almond butter, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Add cocoa powder, almond flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir until well combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips, then spread the batter evenly in the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool before slicing.
  6. Serve and enjoy the plant-based goodness!
